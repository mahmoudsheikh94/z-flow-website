## Keyword Strategy Recommendations

### Priority 1: Low Competition, High Intent
Focus first on these underserved niches:
1. **n8n developer/expert** - Growing demand, low competition
2. **WeWeb developer** - Emerging platform, minimal competition
3. **Xano backend developer** - Niche but growing
4. **Make.com expert Germany** - Localized low competition
5. **MVP Entwicklung Berlin** - German-localized searches
6. **LLM integration developer** - Technical but growing
7. **fractional CTO Berlin** - Service-model positioning

### Priority 2: Medium Competition, High Volume
Build authority on:
1. **MVP development for startups**
2. **no-code development agency**
3. **AI integration services**
4. **workflow automation services**
5. **Prozessautomatisierung** (German)

### Priority 3: Content/SEO Foundation
Create content around problem-aware keywords:
1. "how to build MVP without technical cofounder"
2. "automate business processes without coding"
3. "AI integration for small business"
4. "no-code vs traditional development cost"
5. "Digitalisierung für KMU" (German)

2. **Cost breakdowns**: MVP development pricing guide
